Money Grows on Trees

Profits Grow As Forests Fall

Our planet's ancient forests are disappearing at an alarming rate. These vital ecosystems, which have stood for millennia, are being cleared at a pace of over 10 million hectares per year - equivalent to 27 soccer fields every minute.

Wild forests regulate our climate, purify our air and water, and provide habitat for countless species. Their loss represents one of the greatest environmental challenges of our time.
